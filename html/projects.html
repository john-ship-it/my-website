Projects

In addition to working this past summer, I completed a summer course in AI fundamentals. For the final project, my group and I developed a GRU (type of RNN) to classify news articles as real or fake. The dataset was collected from Kaggle, where it was preprocessed by our code. We then converted the words in each article to GloVe embeddings and fed them into our model. The model reported a final testing accuracy of 98.8%. See github repo here.

Last year, Defense Research and Development Canada (DRDC) asked my group to develop an interface to communicate multiple levels of military information to generals. After many exchanges with the DRDC representative and many iterations of designs, our team delivered our solution to the DRDC in a well-organized report.
